# Privacy Policy for Secret Santa Generator

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Secret Santa Generator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device.** We use local storage (Hive database) to store:
- Event information (names, dates, budgets)
- Participant information (names, emails)
- Secret Santa matches
- Reveal tokens and history

**No data is transmitted to external servers.** All information remains on your device and is never sent to us or any third parties.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal information.** The App operates entirely offline and does not:
- Collect user data
- Track user behavior
- Send analytics
- Use advertising networks
- Share data with third parties

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

**Camera Permission (iOS/Android):**
- Used exclusively for scanning QR codes to reveal Secret Santa matches
- Not used for any other purpose
- You can deny this permission, but QR code scanning will not be available

**Photo Library Permission (iOS):**
- Used to save QR codes to your photo library (optional feature)
- You can deny this permission without affecting core functionality

## Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time by:
- Uninstalling the App (all local data will be removed)
- Clearing app data through device settings
- Using the delete/clear functions within the App

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: support@secretsanta.app

## Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect any information, your use of the App is completely private and secure.
